Our story

Nurturing the Soil,
Empowering Generations

Founded in 2016 in the fertile heart of Gomoa Manso, Central Region, POWERRANGO has grown into Ghana’s benchmark for ethical, high-yield palm oil production. We combine ancestral wisdom with modern agronomy to deliver premium palm oil that respects both people and planet.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional palm products while preserving biodiversity, ensuring deforestation-free supply chains, and uplifting local farmers through fair wages and knowledge transfer. Every drop reflects our promise: quality, integrity, and regeneration.

Premium Palm Oil Production
